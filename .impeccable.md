## Design Context

### Users
Streamers and content creators who broadcast chess games and need engaging visuals for their audience. They use the application during live streams or recorded content where visual appeal and clear communication of game state are crucial for viewer engagement.

### Brand Personality
Retro-futuristic aesthetic that blends vintage computing/terminal aesthetics with futuristic elements. Think 80s sci-fi meets modern chess - neon accents, grid patterns, glowing effects, but with clean, readable typography and functional design.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Color scheme: Dark backgrounds with electric blue, cyan, and magenta accents
- Typography: Monospace-inspired for technical elements, paired with a clean sans-serif for readability
- Visual elements: Subtle grid patterns, glowing effects, CRT-like scanlines (optional), sharp geometric forms
- Theme: Dark mode primary with optional light mode that feels like a "holodeck" simulation

### Design Principles
1. **Clear Visual Hierarchy**: Game state must be instantly readable even at a glance on stream
2. **Retro-Futuristic Authenticity**: Commit to the aesthetic without sacrificing usability
3. **Stream-Friendly Design**: Elements should be visible and meaningful at typical streaming resolutions
4. **AI Coach Integration**: The AI coach should feel like a natural extension of the retro-futuristic theme
5. **Motion with Purpose**: Animations should enhance understanding, not distract from gameplay
